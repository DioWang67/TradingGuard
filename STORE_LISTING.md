### Q: Why do you need access to "Read and change all your data on the websites you visit" (`<all_urls>`)?
**Justification (Update for V4):**
> "The extension provides a universal overlay interface that allows users to perform calculations and take screenshots on ANY website they choose to use for trading analysis (e.g., charts, news, brokerages).
> Because traders use a wide variety of tools and custom URLs, restricting the extension to specific domains would limit its functionality.
> The extension injects a standalone 'shadow DOM' style panel that does not interact with page content other than to capture the visible tab for the user's personal log."
